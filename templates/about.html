ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Onur, a passionate programmer from Istanbul, Turkey, currently studying at the University of Toronto. I've been living in Toronto since 2017. I enjoy building creative and functional software, especially in Python, C, Java, JavaScript, and R. I'm particularly interested in projects that involve graphics, user interaction, and backend systems.

From a young age, I have been drawn to math, physics, and problem solving. With both of my parents working as accountants, I also developed a strong interest in economics. These influences shaped my academic and professional direction. Today, I focus on Software Engineering and Economics, with a growing passion for Data Science where I combine analytical thinking with technical skills to explore meaningful problems.
